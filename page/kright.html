Kris Knight

"You know, the thought process of 'to think the protagonist could actually...'" - Radiation Fox

(2018-2025)

A popular theory from the chapter 2 demo era of the fandom that proposes Kris Dreemurr as the true identity of the Roaring Knight. There were different iterations of the theory but the core was that Kris was the one responsible for creating the dark fountains and that they were playing both sides as the hero and the antagonist in order to fulfill some unknown goal. Some thought it was too obvious to be anything other than a red herring, while others thought it was the most compelling way to subvert Kris' role as the protagonist.

Those times are over, and Kright is officially dead. Now I can only hope to find your trace in dessknight speculation.
